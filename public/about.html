About us

Fresh cookies made for everyday Filipino cravings.

DailyDough.co started as a small home-baking idea: make soft, chewy cookies that feel special enough for gifts but familiar enough for merienda.

Business Story

Like many local Filipino food startups, DailyDough.co began with family taste tests, weekend batches, and orders from friends, classmates, coworkers, and neighbors. The brand focuses on fresh-baked cookies, easy online ordering, and customizable boxes for birthdays, study nights, office treats, and simple pasalubong.

Mission

To serve affordable, crave-worthy cookies with warm service, clear communication, and flavors that fit the Filipino love for sweets, coffee, chocolate, and shareable treats.

Brand Identity

DailyDough.co is friendly, youthful, and homemade at heart. Its warm brown and caramel colors reflect fresh dough, toasted edges, and the comfort of opening a box of cookies made just for you.
